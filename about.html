Our Story

Corner Deli has been a part of the community for over two decades. Founded in 1995 by John and Mary Smith, our deli has been serving delicious sandwiches, salads, and more, made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Our Mission

At Corner Deli, our mission is to provide our customers with wholesome, satisfying meals in a friendly and welcoming environment. We strive to support local farmers and businesses, while also reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices.

Meet the Team

Our team is comprised of passionate individuals dedicated to delivering exceptional service and quality food to our customers. From our chefs crafting delicious dishes to our friendly staff serving with a smile, we are here to make your dining experience memorable.
